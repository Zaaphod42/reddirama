Reddirama

A fullscreen, hands-free slideshow for Reddit: play any subreddit or profile, your Home feed, or (logged in) your saved, upvoted and custom feeds. To use it, install the userscript, then launch it from Reddit:

  1. Install the Userscripts app (iOS/Safari) or Tampermonkey (Chrome).
  2. Add the script from the repo: github.com/Zaaphod42/reddirama
  3. Open reddit.com and click Reddirama.
