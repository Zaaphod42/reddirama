# Privacy Policy

**Reddirama does not collect, store, sell, or transmit any personal data.**

- The extension (and the userscript) run only on **reddit.com**. They add a launch button and, when you click it, read the Reddit listing you choose (a subreddit or profile, your Home feed, or your saved, upvoted and custom feeds) using **your existing Reddit session in your own browser**.
- Those posts are handed to the viewer page (<https://zaaphod42.github.io/reddirama/>) through the browser's `postMessage`, **entirely client-side**, only to display them as a slideshow. They are **not** sent to any server, logged, or stored.
- There is **no account, no extra login, no analytics, and no third-party tracking**. Nothing leaves your browser except the normal requests your browser already makes to Reddit.
- The viewer is a **static page hosted on GitHub Pages**; it has no backend and receives none of your data.

Questions or concerns: <https://github.com/Zaaphod42/reddirama/issues>
